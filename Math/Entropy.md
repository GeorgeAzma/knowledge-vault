Measures information gained or surprise of a random variable
### Formula $\log_2p(x)$
- $p(x)$ Probability of event happening

> [!example] Fair coin flip is 1 bit of information
> Because it could've only had 2 states both equally likely
> **Formula** $\log_22^N=N$ `N is number of flips`

> [!note] Other log bases can also be used $e$, $10$ etc.
