
### Example $\dfrac{1}{n(n+1)}=\dfrac{A}{n}+\dfrac{B}{n+1}$
- $\dfrac{1}{n(n+1)}n(n+1)=\dfrac{A}{n}n(n+1)+\dfrac{B}{n+1}n(n+1)$
- $1=A(n+1)+Bn$
- If $n=-1$ then $A(-1+1)+B(-1)=1$
	- $B(-1)=1$
	- $B=-1$
- If $n=0$ then $A(0+1)+B\cdot0=1$
	- $A=1$
- so $\dfrac{1}{n(n+1)}=\dfrac{1}{n}+\dfrac{-1}{n+1}$
**Answer** $\dfrac{1}{n(n+1)}=\dfrac{1}{n}-\dfrac{1}{n+1}$
