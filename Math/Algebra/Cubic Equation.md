### $ax^3+bx^2+cx+d$
### Discriminant $D=b^2-3ac$
### Cardano's Formula
- $x_1=\sqrt[3]{-\dfrac{d}{2}+\sqrt{\dfrac{d^2}{4}+\dfrac{c^3}{27}}}+\sqrt[3]{-\dfrac{d}{2}-\sqrt{\dfrac{d^2}{4}+\dfrac{c^3}{27}}}-\dfrac{b}{3a}$
- $x_2=-\dfrac{x_1+1}{2}+\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}(x_1-1)i$
- $x_3=-\dfrac{x_1+1}{2}-\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}(x_1-1)i$
### Vieta's Formula
- $x_1+x_2+x_3=-\dfrac{b}{a}$ 
- $x_1x_2+x_1x_3+x_2x_3=\dfrac{c}{a}$  
- $x_1x_2x_3=-\dfrac{d}{a}$ 
### Valley Points
- $x_0=\dfrac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^{2}-3ac}}{3a}$
- $y_0=ax_0^3+bx_0^2+cx_0+d$`
