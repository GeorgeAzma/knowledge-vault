### $ax^3+bx^2+cx+d$
### Discriminant $D=b^2-3ac$
### Cardano's Formula
- $\huge x_1=\sqrt[3]{-\frac{d}{2}+\sqrt{\frac{d^2}{4}+\frac{c^3}{27}}}+\sqrt[3]{-\frac{d}{2}-\sqrt{\frac{d^2}{4}+\frac{c^3}{27}}}-\frac{b}{3a}$
- $\huge x_2=-\frac{x_1+1}{2}+\frac{\sqrt3}{2}(x_1-1)i$
- $\huge x_3=-\frac{x_1+1}{2}-\frac{\sqrt3}{2}(x_1-1)i$
### Vieta's Formula
- $\large x_1+x_2+x_3=-\huge\frac{b}{a}$ 
- $\large x_1x_2+x_1x_3+x_2x_3=\huge\frac{c}{a}$  
- $\large x_1x_2x_3=-\huge\frac{d}{a}$ 
### Valley Points
- $\large x_0=\huge\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^{2}-3ac}}{3a}$
- $\large y_0=ax_0^3+bx_0^2+cx_0+d$`
