## $\phi=1.6180339887498948482$
## $\phi=\dfrac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2}=1+\dfrac{1}{1+\dfrac{1}{1+\dfrac{1}{\dots}}}$
`Seen in nature, can be used to create organic and aesthetically pleasing composition`
![[Golden Ratio.webp]]
### $\dfrac{a+b}{a}=\dfrac{a}{b}$
### Properties
- $\dfrac{1}{\phi}=\phi-1$
- $\phi^2=\phi+1$
- $1+\dfrac{1}{\phi}=\phi$
- $\phi^2-\phi-1=0$

> [!note] Used in vogel's method, which distributes points almost evenly on a circle/sphere

> [!note] There is also silver ratio $\dfrac{2a+b}{a}=\dfrac{a}{b}$ which is $1+\sqrt{2}$
> And generalization called metallic ratio $\dfrac{na+b}{a}=\dfrac{a}{b}$ which is $\dfrac{n+\sqrt{n^2+4}}{2}=n+\dfrac{1}{n+\dfrac{1}{n+\dfrac{1}{\dots}}}$
