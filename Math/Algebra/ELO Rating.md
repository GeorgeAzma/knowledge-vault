### Formula $R'=R+K(S-\frac1{1+10^\frac{R_{opponent}-R}{400}})$
- $R',R$ new/old rating
- $K=24,S=1$
### Simplified  $R'=R+24-\dfrac{24}{1+10^\dfrac{R_{opponent}-R}{400}}$
> [!example] $R=2063,R_{opponent}=2120$
> $R'=2063+24-\dfrac{24}{1+10^\dfrac{2120-2063}{400}}=2087-\dfrac{24}{1+10^\dfrac{36}{400}}=2087-\dfrac{24}{1+10^{0.09}}=2087-\dfrac{24}{2.23}\approx2076$
