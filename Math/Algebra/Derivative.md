> [!note] Notations $\large \frac{\delta f}{\delta x}=\frac{d}{dx} f(x)=f'(x)$ 
### Finite Difference Method $f(x+h)-f(x-h) \over 2h$ or $f((x+h)-f(h)) \over h$
[[Partial Derivative]]
### Simplifications
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}c=0$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}(a+b)=a'+b'$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}(ab)=ab'+ba'$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}(\frac{a}{b})=\frac{a'b-ab'}{b^2}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}{\large\frac{1}{a}}=-\frac{a'}{a^2}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}(c\cdot a)=c\cdot a'$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}x^n=n\cdot x^{n-1}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}y^x=\ln{y}e^{x\ln{y}}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}f(g(x))=f'(g(x))\cdot g'(x)$ [[Chain Rule]]
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\sin(x)=\cos(x)$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\cos(x)=-\sin(x)$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\sqrt{x}=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\log_b{x}=\frac{1}{\ln(b)x}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}e^x=e^x$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\tan{x}=\sec^2(x)$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\arcsin(x)=\frac1{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\arccos(x)=-\frac1{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$
- $\frac{d}{dx}\arctan(x)=\large\frac1{1+x^2}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\ln{x}=\large\frac1{x}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\log_b{x}=\frac{1}{\ln(b)x}=\frac{\log_b(e)}{x}$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\sinh{x}=\cosh x$
- $\large\frac{d}{dx}\cosh{x}=\sinh{x}$
