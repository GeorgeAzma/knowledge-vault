### Formula $M^{-1}=\frac{adj(M)}{\det(M)}$
- Calculate [[Adjugate]] `transposed cofactor matrix`
- Divide result by [[Determinant]] `if det=0, then no inverse`
### Properties
- $AA^{-1}=I$ [[Identity Matrix]]
- $(A^T)^{-1}=(A^{-1})^T$
- $(AB)^{-1}=B^{-1}A^{-1}$
- $A^{-n}=(A^{-1})^n$
- $(\lambda A)^{-1}=\large\frac{A^{-1}}{\lambda}$
- [[Diagonal Matrix]] `diag = 1 / diag`
- [[Symmetric Matrix]] inverse is [[Symmetric Matrix]]
- $\begin{pmatrix}A&0\\0&B\end{pmatrix}^{-1}=\begin{pmatrix}A^{-1}&0\\0&B^{-1}\end{pmatrix}$
- $AXB=C\implies X=A^{-1}CB^{-1}$
