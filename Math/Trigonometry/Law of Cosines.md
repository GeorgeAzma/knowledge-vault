### Formula $c=\sqrt{a^2+b^2-2ab\cos\gamma}$
![[Law of Cosines.webp]]
#### Other Formulas
- $c^2=a^2+b^2-2ab\cos\gamma$ `for finding sides`
- $\cos \alpha=\frac{b^2+c^2-a^2}{2bc}$ `for finding angles`
- $|\vec{x}+\vec{y}|=\sqrt{|\vec{x}|^2+|\vec{y}|^2+2|\vec{x}||\vec{y}|\cos\theta}$ 
    - finds length of vector sum using only their lengths and angle between them
    - equivalent to $x=(|\vec{x}|,0),\ y=|\vec{y}|(\cos\theta,\sin\theta)$ and then $|x+y|$
