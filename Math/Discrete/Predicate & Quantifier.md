### Predicate $P(x)=\text{x is even}$
statement containing variables
becomes **proposition** when variable is replaced with a specific value
### Quantifier
- $\forall x\in\mathbb{N},~P(x)$ **universal quantifier** `forall x in natural numbers that is even`
- $\exists x\in N,~P(x)$ **existential quantifier** `exists x in natural numbers that is even`
shows if something is true for all or some members of the group
