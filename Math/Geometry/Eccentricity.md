### $e=c/a$
- $c$ focal distance
- $a$ major-axis
### Cases
 - $e=0~~~~~~~$ [[Circle]] `focus = center, focal distance = 0`
 - $0<e<1$ [[Ellipse]] `focal distance < major-axis`
 - $e=1~~~~~~~$ [[Parabola]] `focal distance = major-axis / directrix`
 - $e>1~~~~~~~$ [[Hyperbola]] `focal distance > major axis`
