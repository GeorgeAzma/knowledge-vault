### $\dfrac{x^2}{a^2}-\dfrac{y^2}{b^2}=1$
- $a>b$
- **Foci** $(\pm c,0)$
- $c=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}$
- **Vertices** $(\pm a,0)$
- [[Eccentricity]] $e=\dfrac{c}{e}>1$
- [[Directrix|Directrices]] $x=\pm\dfrac{a}{e}=a^2/c$
- **Tangent Slope** at point $(x,y)$ on hyperbola $y'=\dfrac{b^2x}{a^2y}$
### Properties
- If you send a ray toward one focus, after bouncing off the curve, it will pass through the other focus
- $|d_1-d_2|=2a$ where $d_1,d_2$ are distance to right/left focus
