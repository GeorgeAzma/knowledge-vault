take a point on parameteric function `graph`, and draw a circle 
that has outer curvature matching the curvature of the function
$1 / \text{radius}$ of that circle is the curvature $\large\kappa$ of the function `which is signed`
### 2D $\kappa=\dfrac{f_x'f_y''-f_y'f_x''}{||f'||^3}$
### 3D $\kappa=\dfrac{||f'\times f''||}{||f'||^3}$
### [[Geometric Algebra]] Generalization $\kappa=\dfrac{f'\wedge f''}{||f'||^3}$
