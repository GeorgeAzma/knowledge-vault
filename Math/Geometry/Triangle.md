[[Right Angle Triangle]]
### Area $\sqrt{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)}$
- $a,b,c$ Side lengths
- $s=\huge\frac{a+b+c}{2}$
- $s=\huge\frac{hc}{2}$ `h is height, c is base length`
- $s=\huge\frac{ac\sin\alpha}{2}$ `a,c are adjacent side lengths to angle alpha`
