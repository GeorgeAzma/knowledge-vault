### Second Order Curve $Ax^2+Bxy+Cy^2+Dx+Ey+F=0$
- $\nabla=B^2-4AC$ `discriminant`
    - $<0$ [[Ellipse]]
    - $=0$ [[Parabola]]
    - $>0$ [[Hyperbola]]
- $\tan2\theta=\dfrac{B}{A-C}$ `rotation`
### [[Circle]] $Ax^2+Cy^2+F=0$
- $A=C>0$
- $B=0$ no rotation
- $D=E=0$ centered at origin
- $F=-r^2$
### [[Ellipse]] $Ax^2+Cy^2+F=0$
- $B=0$ no rotation
- $D=E=0$ centered at origin
- $a=\dfrac{F}{\sqrt{A}},\quad b=\dfrac{F}{\sqrt{C}}$
