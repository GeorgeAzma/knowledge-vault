### Formula $\sum ar^n=\dfrac{a}{1-r}$ where $|r|<1$
>[!example] $a=1$ and $r=0.2$
>$1+0.2+0.2\cdot0.2+0.2\cdot0.2\cdot0.2=0.2+0.04+0.008=1.248$
>Converges to $1.25$
### Nth Sum $S_n=\dfrac{a(1-r^n)}{1-r}$
> [!example] $a=1,~r=2,~n=6$
> - $S_6=\dfrac{1-2^6}{1-2}=\dfrac{-63}{-1}=63$
>- $S_6=\sum_0^{\log_2(n)-1}=2^0+2^1+2^3+2^4+2^5=1+2+4+8+16+32=63$
