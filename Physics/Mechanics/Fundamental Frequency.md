### Cantilever Beam Formula $f=\dfrac{\sqrt{\frac{3EI}{mL^{3}}}}{2\pi}$
- $E$ [[Young's Modulus]]
- $I=\dfrac{wt^3}{12}$ cross-sectional [[Moment of Inertia]] `for rectangular beam`
- $L$ beam length
- $m$ mass per unit area `total mass divided by L`
> [!note] Stiffness $k=\dfrac{3EI}{L^3}$

> [!example] steel ruler oscillation over the desk 
> - $E=200\times 10^9$
> - $w=2cm=0.02m$
> - $t=1mm=0.001m$ `thickness`
> - $I=\dfrac{0.02*0.001^3}{12}=\dfrac{2*10^{-11}}{3}$
> - $L=20cm=0.2$
> - $\rho=7850kg/m^3$ `steel density`
> - $m=wt\rho$
> - $f=\dfrac{\sqrt{\dfrac{3\cdot200\cdot10^{9}\cdot0.02\cdot0.001^{3}\cdot12^{-1}}{0.02\cdot0.001\cdot7850\cdot0.2^{3}}}}{2\pi}\approx4.49hz$
### String Formula $f=\sqrt{\dfrac{T}{4Lm}}$
- $T$ string tension `in newtons`
- $L$ string length
- $m$ string mass
