### Formula
- $x_1,x_2$ position
- $v_1,v_2$ velocity
- $m_1,m_2$ mass
- $\text{impulse}=\dfrac{2(v_1-v_2)\cdot(x_1-x_2)}{(m_1+m_2)|x_1-x_2|^2}(x_1-x_2)$
- $v'_1=v_1-m_2\cdot\text{impulse}$
- $v'_2=v_2+m_1\cdot\text{impulse}$ 
> [!tip] Particle's [[Kinetic Energy]] before and after collision is the same
