### Formula $L=mvr=pr$
- $r$ radius/distance from the pivot point
- Linear momentum $p=mv$
	- $v$ Angular velocity `perpendicular to spinning beam`
- Moment of inertia $I=mr=\large\frac{L}v$
- Torque $\tau=r\times F$
