- **Spectral roll-off** frequency below which `κ = 90%` of signal resides `magnitude based`
- **Spectral flatness** how peaked/flat audio spectrum is `dB based`
     ratio of [[Mean|Geometric Mean]] by [[Mean|Arithmetic Mean]] of the power spectrum components within a subband
     `0 = pure tone` and `1 = noisy`
- **Spectral entropy** apply [[Entropy|Shannon Entropy]] to normalized power spectral density
     `low SE value = predictable signal / pure tone` and `high SE value = random signal`
- **Spectral centroid** amplitude weighted average of frequencies
- **Crest Factor** `peak / average`
- **Fundamental** lowest frequency harmonic wave
- **Harmonic Ratio** harmonic energy divided by the total energy
