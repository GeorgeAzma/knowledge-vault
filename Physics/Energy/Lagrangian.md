### Formula $L=T-V$
- $T$ [[Kinetic Energy]]
- $V$ [[Potential Energy]]
### Euler-Lagrange Equation $\dfrac{d}{dt}(\dfrac{\delta L}{\delta \dot{x}})=\dfrac{\delta L}{\delta x}$
> [!example] [[Spring]] $m\ddot{x}=-kx$, finding using lagrangian
> $L=\dfrac{m\dot{x}^2}{2}-\dfrac{kx^2}{2}$
> $\dfrac{d}{dt}(\dfrac{\delta L}{\delta \dot{x}})=\dfrac{\delta L}{\delta x}$
> $\dfrac{d}{dt}(m\dot{x})=-kx$
> $m\ddot{x}=-kx$
### Intuition
Universe minimizes energy, so minimizing $L$ minimizes [[Kinetic Energy]] 
and maximizes [[Potential Energy]] for energy conservation
