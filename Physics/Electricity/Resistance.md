### Formula $R=\large\frac{V}{I}$
- $V$ [[Voltage]]
- $I$ [[Current]]
**Resistors in Series** $R=R_1+R_2+R_3$
**Resistors in Parallel** $R=\large\frac{1}{R_1^{-1}+R_2^{-1}+R_3^{-1}}$
> [!example] Three $100\ohm$ resistors
> - $R=100+100+100=300$
> - $R=1/(1/100+1/100+1/100)=1/(3/100)=100/3\approx33\ohm$
