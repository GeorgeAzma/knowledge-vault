Converts numbers to sounds (usually 2 number chunks)
Used to generate [[Person Action Object]]
### Mapping:
- 0 = s, z
- 1 = t, d
- 2 = n
- 3 = m
- 4 = R
- 5 = L
- 6 = sh, j, ch
- 7 = K
- 8 = f, v
- 9 = p, b
### Convert Words to Numbers
- Convert first 2 consonants to numbers
- Ignore W,H,Y consonants
Major -> mj -> 36
