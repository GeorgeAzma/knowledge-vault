Chinese alphabet of words which is used in [[Japanese]] 
`with slight modifications`

- **On-yomi** is borrowed chinese reading 
- **Kun-yomi** is japanese reading, when kanji has hiragana attached
  it almost always is **Kun-yomi** reading 
- First pronounciation is always on-yomi others are kun-yomi
