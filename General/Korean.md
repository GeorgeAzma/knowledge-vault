### Structure
**syllable block** = **consonant + Vowel `+ final consonant`**
- 가 = ㄱ `g` + ㅏ `a` → "ga"  
- 한 = ㅎ `h` + ㅏ `a` + ㄴ `n` → "han"  
- 글 = ㄱ `g` + ㅡ `eu` + ㄹ `l` → "geul"
